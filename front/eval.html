Metric definitions

Precision@K

From K items shown, how many are relevant. 7/10 = 0.70.

#relevant in top-K / K

Recall@K

Of all items user would like, how many appear in top-K.

#relevant in top-K / total relevant

NDCG@K

Order-aware precision — relevant item at rank 1 scores more than rank 9.

DCG / ideal DCG

MRR

Average 1/rank of the first relevant item. High = relevant appears early.

avg(1 / rank_first_relevant)

Diversity

Fraction of unique cities + types in results. Prevents filter bubble.

(unique_cities + unique_types) / 2K

Coverage

Unique IDs recommended across all users / total catalog (~26k).

#unique_ids / 26000
Full hybrid scoring formula
final_score = 0.4 × similarity

            − 0.02 × diversity_penalty
